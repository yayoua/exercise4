Photo Gallery: Women in K-pop

Chungha

Chungha (청하) is a South Korean singer, dancer, songwriter, and choreographer.
She became well known after finishing on rank 4 on the survival show Produce 101 and became part of the girl group I.O.I. She officially debuted as a solo artist on June 7, 2017, under MNH Entertainment.

Hwasa

Ahn Hye-jin (born July 23, 1995), better known by her stage name Hwasa, is a South Korean singer, songwriter and rapper signed under RBW Entertainment.
She is a member of South Korean girl group Mamamoo.

IU

Lee Ji-eun (Korean: 이지은; born May 16, 1993), professionally known as IU (Korean: 아이유), is a South Korean singer-songwriter and actress.

Seulgi

Kang Seul-gi (born February 10, 1994), better known simply as Seulgi, is a South Korean singer.
She is a member of South Korean girl group Red Velvet.

Sunmi

Lee Sun-mi (born Sun Mi on May 2, 1992), known mononymously as Sunmi, is a South Korean singer.
She debuted in 2007 as a member of South Korean girl group Wonder Girls and left the group in January 2010 to pursue an academic career.
